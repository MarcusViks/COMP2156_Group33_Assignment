Course Objectives: 
 > Understand DevOps Fundamentals:
   - Explore the core principles and concepts of DevOps.
   - Gain insights into the cultural shift and collaborative nature of DevOps practices.
 >  Automation and Toolchain:
   - Learn about the automation of build, test, and deployment processes.
   - Explore popular DevOps tools such as Jenkins, Git, Docker, and Ansible.
 > Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery (CI/CD):
   - Implement CI/CD pipelines to achieve faster and more reliable software releases.
   - Understand the importance of continuous integration and delivery in the DevOps context.
