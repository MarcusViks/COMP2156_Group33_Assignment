George Brown College is a public post-secondary institution located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.
The college offers a range of student services, including academic advising, career services, counseling, and accessibility services, among others.
The college provides a variety of programs, including degree programs, diplomas, certificates, and apprenticeships, in areas such as Arts, Design & Information Technology, Business, Community Services, Construction & Engineering Technologies, Health Sciences, Hospitality & Culinary Arts, and Preparatory & Liberal Studies
The college provides online courses and programs for students who prefer distance learning.
