COMP 2156, titled "DevOps For System Administration," is a course within the Computer Systems Technology program at George Brown College.
The Computer Systems Technology program at George Brown College is designed with extensive industry input and offers two optional specializations: Networking and Systems. 
The Systems specialization includes courses on mail server administration, content management systems, database administration, and computer security, among other.
