# Importance of learning DevOps software development cycle

There are many reasons to why DevOps is important in software development.


Here are a couple of points:

- Imporved Team Collaboration

- Scalability

- Increased Reliability

- Cost Efficiency



In general, DevOps is a crucial skill to learn, especially in a software driven field.

It is an In-Demand skill set, along with allowing people to advance their career at a fast pace.

It is fairly future-proof. and also teaches skills that can help someone stay adaptable.
